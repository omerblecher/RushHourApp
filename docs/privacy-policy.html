Privacy Policy

App: Rush Hour Puzzle
Platform: Android
Last updated: April 2026

Information We Collect

Authentication

We use Google Firebase Authentication to allow users to sign in. When you sign in, we store your email address and display name to identify your account.

Gameplay Data

We use Google Firestore to store your puzzle progress — including win counts and high scores — associated with your user account.

Advertising

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use the following data to serve personalized ads:

  • Advertising ID — a resettable device identifier used to serve relevant ads and measure ad performance
  • Device information — device type, OS version, and general location (country/region) used for ad targeting
  • App usage data — which screens you visit and when, used to improve ad relevance

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you will be asked for consent before personalized advertising is enabled. You can revisit your consent choice at any time via the Privacy Settings option in the app.

For more information on how Google uses this data, see Google's advertising policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Data shared with Google (Firebase, AdMob) is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

Account and gameplay data is retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion by contacting us at the email below. Ad-related data collected by AdMob is retained per Google's data retention policies.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
