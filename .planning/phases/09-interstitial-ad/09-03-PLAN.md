  <what-built>
The Phase 9 interstitial ad system is now live in the running app:
- adService preloads an interstitial on GameScreen mount (Plan 01 + Plan 02 useEffect)
- Win counter increments on every `showInterstitialIfDue()` call
- Every 3rd win triggers the interstitial; other wins do nothing
- A 5-second timeout guarantees the WinModal navigation always completes
- App restart resets the counter (module-scope variable)
  </what-built>
  <how-to-verify>
Perform each of the 5 checks below in order. Open `adb logcat | grep -iE "admob|interstitial"` in a separate terminal so you can see the load events.

**Check 1 — INTER-02 (every 3rd win, not earlier):**
1. Open the app, navigate to a Beginner puzzle
2. Solve it (or use the simplest puzzle to win quickly)
3. Wait for the WinModal to appear, then tap "Next Puzzle"
4. EXPECTED: NO interstitial. Navigation goes directly to the next puzzle.
5. Solve the second puzzle. Tap "Next Puzzle" on the WinModal.
6. EXPECTED: NO interstitial. Direct navigation again.
7. Solve the third puzzle. Tap "Next Puzzle" on the WinModal.
8. EXPECTED: A Google test interstitial appears (full-screen "Test Ad" with a Close X). After tapping the X, navigation completes to the next puzzle.

**Check 2 — INTER-03 (auto-reload after dismiss):**
9. Continue solving puzzles (4, 5).
10. Solve the 6th puzzle and tap "Next Puzzle" or "Back to Selection".
11. EXPECTED: Another interstitial appears (proves the reload-on-Dismissed worked).

**Check 3 — INTER-04 (timeout guard / failure resilience):**
12. Enable Airplane Mode on the device (turn off WiFi + cellular data).
13. Force-close the app (swipe from recents).
14. Reopen the app and navigate to a puzzle. Solve 3 puzzles in a row.
15. On the 3rd win, tap "Next Puzzle".
16. EXPECTED: Either no ad appears AND navigation completes within ~5 seconds, OR an ad appears (cached) and dismisses normally. The app must NOT hang. The WinModal must NOT block.
17. Disable Airplane Mode before continuing.

**Check 4 — INTER-05 (session reset):**
18. Force-close the app from recents.
19. Reopen the app and solve 1 puzzle.
20. Tap "Next Puzzle" on the WinModal.
21. EXPECTED: NO interstitial (the counter started at 0 again, this is win #1 not win #4).

**Check 5 — INTER-02 boundary (Back to Selection trigger):**
22. From the puzzle list, start fresh. Solve 3 puzzles. On the 3rd WinModal, tap "Back to Selection" (NOT "Next Puzzle").
23. EXPECTED: Interstitial appears, then navigation goes to the puzzle selection screen.

If any of the 5 checks fail, describe the failure (which check, what happened, what was expected) and the planner will reopen Plan 01 or 02.
  </how-to-verify>
