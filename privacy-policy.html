Privacy Policy

Rush Hour Puzzle  ·  Last updated: March 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Rush Hour Puzzle app ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information. We are committed to collecting only what is necessary to provide the service and never selling your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information needed to operate the app:

  • Anonymous account identifier — when you play as a guest, Firebase Authentication automatically creates an anonymous account with a random unique ID. No name, email, or personal details are collected.
  • Google account information — if you choose to sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google. This is used solely to identify your account and is not shared with third parties.
  • Puzzle scores — your best move count per puzzle is stored in Firebase Firestore, linked to your account ID.

We do not collect location data, contacts, device identifiers, payment information, or any other personal data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To save and restore your puzzle progress across sessions
  • To display your best scores within the app
  • To allow you to upgrade from a guest account to a Google account without losing progress

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase Authentication (Google LLC) — manages sign-in. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Cloud Firestore (Google LLC) — stores puzzle scores. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In (Google LLC) — optional authentication method. Google Privacy Policy

4. Data Retention

Anonymous account data is retained until the app is uninstalled or the account is deleted. Google-linked account data is retained until you request deletion. To delete your data, contact us at the email below.

5. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Guest play requires no personal information. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

6. Data Security

All data is stored in Google Firebase, which uses industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. We apply Firebase Security Rules to ensure users can only access their own data.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Play entirely as a guest with no personal data collected

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
